Heidelberg Machine Learning Network Event

More than 20 research groups in Heidelberg work on ML - would you have guessed?

In conjunction with Prof. Carsten Rother (Head of Visual Learning Lab Heidelberg) we are currently planning an event that will provide a stage for ML-related research groups to present their research, challenges and future work. The event will take place on

28 November, 1:30pm - 7pm
HS1 INF 301, Physics Building, Neuenheimer Feld, Heidelberg

For contributions please e-mail to Carsten Rother.

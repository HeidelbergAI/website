heidelberg.ai is a non-profit platform for enthusiasts, students and researchers in the field of Artificial Intelligence. We connect over 500 members.

We provide opportunities for technical discussions, networking and connecting with industrial partners. Our vision is to create a hub of AI expertise in the Heidelberg area. People with all backgrounds and knowledge levels are welcome to participate.

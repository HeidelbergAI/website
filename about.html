heidelberg.ai is a platform for enthusiasts, students and researchers in the field of Artificial Intelligence.

We provide opportunities for technical discussions, networking and connecting with industrial partners. Our vision is to create a hub of AI expertise in the Heidelberg area. People with all backgrounds and knowledge levels are welcome to participate.

heidelberg.ai is organized by @saakohl and @pfjaeger.

Thanks to our host, the Division of Medical Image Computing at DKFZ, attendance and other goodies are free of charge!

The meetup is further supported by the Visual Learning Lab Heidelberg and the Division of Computer Assisted Medical Interventions at DKFZ.


Please contact us for ideas, contributions and event suggestions at info@heidelberg.ai .
